Christmas Traditions Around the World

Christmas is celebrated differently across various cultures, each with unique customs and traditions.

United Kingdom:
The British celebrate with Christmas crackers, mince pies, and the Queen's speech. Boxing Day on December 26th is also an important holiday.

United States:
Americans emphasize family gatherings, gift exchanges, and elaborate decorations. Santa Claus and his reindeer are central to the celebration.

IELTS Academic Writing Task 2

Today we'll discuss effective strategies for IELTS Writing Task 2. The key to success lies in understanding the question types and structuring your essay properly.

Main Question Types:
1. Opinion Essays - Do you agree or disagree?
2. Discussion Essays - Discuss both views
3. Problem-Solution Essays
4. Advantage-Disadvantage Essays

Essential Structure:
- Introduction: Paraphrase the question and state your position
- Body Paragraph 1: First main idea with examples
- Body Paragraph 2: Second main idea with examples
- Conclusion: Summarize your points

Remember to:
✓ Use a variety of vocabulary
✓ Include complex sentence structures
✓ Provide specific examples
✓ Maintain formal academic tone
✓ Aim for 250+ words

Practice regularly and analyze model answers to improve your skills.
